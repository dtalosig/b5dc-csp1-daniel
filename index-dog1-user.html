This incredible story of a regular woman doing something extraordinary will make your day and restore your faith in humanity.

Cher Herrera is 38 and is a customer service representative who works from her home in Bacolod City in the Philippines. She lives in a modest 86 square meter house (about 925 square feet) with her brother, as well as her two nieces and nephew, whom she cares for. Herrera is also an avid dog lover, and has two rescue Golden Retrievers, Echo and Faith.
